Which GoPro®s are compatible with Solo?

GoPro® - HERO4 Black

  • Supports video transmission

  • Stabilization optimized without counterweights

  • Start/Stop recording available via the 3DR Solo app if you download special firmware from GoPro®. It’s version v2.50.00 and has not yet been released to the public at this time. Latest public version is v2.00.00. (Check current version number on the GoPro® screen when you turn it on).

  • Remaining GoPro® settings coming soon.

GoPro® - HERO4 Silver

  • Same capabilities as the GoPro® Hero 4 Black, but stabilization is optimized with the 4-silver counterweights included with the Gimbal

GoPro® - HERO3+ Black

  • Supports video transmission

  • Stabilization optimized with HERO3+ Black counterweights included with the gimbal

  • Start/Stop recording available via the 3DR Solo app with publicly released FW v3.00.00

  • Remaining GoPro® settings coming soon

GoPro® - HERO3+ Silver

  • Supports video transmission

  • Stabilization optimized with HERO3+ Silver counterweights included with the gimbal

  • Start/Stop recording and other GoPro® control settings not available

GoPro® - HERO3 Black or Silver

  • Video transmission

  • Will fit in the Solo Gimbal, but stabilization is not optimized for the weight of this camera

  • Start/Stop recording and other GoPro® control settings not available

Any other camera with HDMI-out

  • Video transmission should work

  • Not compatible with the Solo gimbal, but the Open Gimbal Bay is designed for third party innovation
